Properties of exponents:

`a^n*a^m=a^(n+m)`

`a^n/a^m=a^(n-m)`

`(a^n)^m=a^(n*m)`

`a^n*b^n=(a*b)^n`

`a^n/b^n=(a/b)^n`

Example:

`4^4/2^5=(2^2)^4/2^5=2^8/2^5=2^(8-5)=2^3`

Factorial:

`n! =1*2*3*...*(n-1)*n`

Example:

`5! =1*2*3*4*5=120`

Limit of a sequence:

`lim_(n->oo)a_n`

Example:

`lim_(n->oo)sqrt((9n^2-3)/(4n^2+1))=lim_(n->oo)sqrt((n^2(9-3/n^2))/(n^2(4+1/n^2)))=lim_(n->oo)sqrt((9-3/n^2)/(4+1/n^2))=sqrt((9-0)/(4+0))=sqrt(9/4)=3/2`

Integral:

`intf(x)dx=F(x)+C`

Example:

`inte^(2x)dx=((2x=t),(x=1/2t),(dx=1/2dt))=1/2inte^tdt=1/2e^t+C=1/2e^(2x)+C`

Definite Integral:

`int_a^bf(x)dx=[F(x)]_a^b=F(b)-F(a)`

Example:

`int_0^1xdx=[1/2x^2]_0^1=(1/2*1^2)-(1/2*0^2)=1/2-0=1/2`
